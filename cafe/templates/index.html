Serving Since 1950

Our Story

Serving happiness since 1950, rooted in the heart of Mumbai near Juhu.

Our journey began in 1950 in Mumbai, near the serene shores of Juhu. What started as a small café has now grown into a cherished place where flavors meet tradition. Over the decades, we have perfected our recipes, welcoming generations of food lovers with warm hospitality and timeless taste. Our commitment to quality and authenticity remains unchanged, ensuring every bite is a tribute to our legacy.

Our Vision

Since 1950, our vision has been to create a space where flavors, culture, and hospitality blend seamlessly. Rooted in Mumbai near Juhu, we strive to preserve tradition while embracing innovation, ensuring that every guest experiences warmth, authenticity, and excellence.
